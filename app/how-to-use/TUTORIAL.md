# Interactive Tutorial - Getting Started with Forza Color Universe

## Welcome! 🎨

This interactive guide will help you explore 10,000+ official Forza paint colors.

## Step 1: Browse Colors

1. **View the Gallery**: Scroll through the color grid on the home page
2. **Click any color card** to see detailed HSB values
3. **Favorite colors** by clicking the ⭐ icon

## Step 2: Search & Filter

### Quick Search

```
Type in the search box:
- "Ferrari" - Find all Ferrari colors
- "Red" - Find red-toned paints
- "Matte" - Find matte finish colors
```

### Advanced Filters

- **Manufacturer**: Select from 200+ brands
- **Color Type**: Metal Flake, Normal, Two-Tone, Matte, Carbon Fiber
- **Favorites**: Click "FAVORITES" to view saved colors

## Step 3: Use Tools

### 🎨 Paint Scanner

1. Click "Upload Image"
2. Select a car photo
3. Extract dominant colors
4. Find matching Forza paints

### 🎰 Color Roulette

1. Click "Spin" for random colors
2. Generate harmony palettes (Complementary, Triadic, Analogous)
3. Discover new color combinations

### 🔧 TuneForge

1. Click the TuneForge button (bottom right)
2. Select your car
3. Get AI-powered tuning recommendations

## Step 4: Export & Share

- **Copy HSB Values**: Click color card → Copy button
- **Share Colors**: Use share button on color details
- **Export Favorites**: Download your collection as JSON

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- `T` - Toggle dark/light theme
- `S` - Focus search box
- `Esc` - Close popups

## Tips & Tricks

💡 **Pro Tip**: Use the Paint Scanner with screenshots from Forza to find exact color matches!

💡 **Performance**: Virtual scrolling handles 10,000+ colors smoothly

💡 **Offline**: Works offline after first load (PWA)
